Get Weird With It

A poster I made using Adobe Photoshop. I originally wanted to style it as a modern-day lineless minimalist design with a slight psychedelic touch of themes present in artwork of the '60s/'70s, but the rough outlines I drew looked so much better, so I met in the middle and went for the retro neon-florescent wireframe design that vaguely blends the '80s with the '90s.

urban nights

Drawn in Krita. The city is as busy as it is vibrant, and that is especially true when it gets dark.
This is why glass elevators are an unspoken wonder: you're inside, yet you're high off the ground, surrounded in that beautiful vibrance.
It's a very odd, yet special feeling, unless you are afraid of heights, which is fair and I have no reason to judge you for it.
